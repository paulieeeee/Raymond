The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

           Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all other scientific journals her work had appeared in, was in a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly favourite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls she felt she needed to be free.

           She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who having nothing to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling screte of llama DNA, to ber an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
